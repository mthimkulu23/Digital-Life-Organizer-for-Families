Cookie Policy

How we use small text files to run this application safely.

1. Necessary Authentication Cookies

We employ secure session cookies strictly for maintaining user authentication state while logged into the app dashboard. These are mandatory for the service to function.

2. Analytics & Tracking

We currently do not implement cross-site tracking, remarketing cookies, or aggressive analytics pixels that could compromise your privacy while managing sensitive estate data.

3. Cookie Lifespan

Session keys are destroyed immediately upon logging out or closing your browser. They are not stored persistently.

4. Managing Cookies

You can set your browser to refuse all or some browser cookies, or to alert you when websites set or access cookies. If you disable or refuse cookies, please note that the main dashboard and login features will become inaccessible.

Because we value your privacy, we automatically opt you out of all non-essential cookies by default.
