How can we help?

Find answers to common questions about the Digital Life Organizer.

After logging in, go to your Dashboard and click "Add Trusted Contact". Fill in their name, relationship, email, and phone. Once added, they will receive a notification to confirm — but they can only access your vault using your emergency code.

Go to your Dashboard and click "Upload Document" or click the "Upload File" card. Select the document type (e.g. Life Insurance, Last Will), add special instructions, and attach the file. It is immediately encrypted on upload.

Your Emergency Code is a unique 12-character code generated when you register. Store it in a safe place (e.g., written in a sealed envelope with your physical documents). When someone presents this code on the Emergency Access page, they will be granted read-only view of your vault.

Your data is protected with multiple layers of security. All passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms. Document files are encrypted at rest. Access is restricted by session-based authentication and only unlocks through the emergency code pathway.

Only you can access your vault under normal circumstances. Trusted contacts you have added can only gain read-only access by entering your emergency code on the Emergency Access page — no one else can bypass this gate.
