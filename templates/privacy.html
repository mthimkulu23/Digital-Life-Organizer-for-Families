Commitment to Security

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is fundamental to our service design.

1. Information Collection & Encryption

We collect an email address and billing details to administer your account. All other vault data (documents, passwords, wishes, trusted contact data) is encrypted end-to-end. We cannot read, scan, or access your vault contents.

2. Data Usage

We only process your non-encrypted basic account data to provide support, run security updates, and enforce account limitations related to the free and family plans.

3. Trusted Contacts & Emergency Unlocking

When an emergency code is presented by a designated contact, they gain access to the decrypted segments of your account as per your settings. It is your responsibility to share emergency codes securely off-platform.

4. Data Deletion

You have the right to request full account deletion at any time. When deleted, all associated identifying and vault data is permanently purged from our servers.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share billing data strictly with our secure payment processing partner.
